Hi, welcome to our website: “How to play the Guitar”!
We, the creators, are siblings from James Ruse Agricultural High School, who created this website to share our love of music with people all around the world.
To access our guitar lessons, go to the ‘Lessons’ button on the navigation bar. To learn more about us and our musical journey, go to the ‘About Us’ page.
If you found our lessons useful, click ‘Further Links’ on the navigation bar, to find out how you can continue progressing in your guitar journey.
