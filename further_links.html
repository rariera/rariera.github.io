Further Links

Hi! Hope our website helped you learn a lot of the basics about playing the guitar. To help you continue to learn, here are some helpful links you might want to check out!

Artist Guitars
This is a really good online store for any guitar related items you might be needing. They have pretty reasonable prices, too!

Guitar Tuna
This is a tuning app for android devices.

Fender Tune
Or, a guitar tuning app for iphones.

Ultimate Guitar
Not sure what to play? Ultimate guitar is a really good resource to find songs and their chords, which can be used completely free.

Hope these links help you get started if you're stuck for where to go next. Thanks for checking out our website.
