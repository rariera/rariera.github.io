Lesson 1: Guitar Basics

Included in this video will be:
- An introduction to guitar
- How to hold the guitar
- What the names of the strings are
- A fun mneumonic to remember them!

Lesson 2: Other Stuff?

Included in this video will be:
- Some basic chords in the key of G, including:
- G major chord (tonic)
- C major chord (subdominant)
- D major chord (dominant)
- E minor chord (minor sixth)

Lesson 3: Putting it together!

In this lesson, we'll basically just be seeing how the chords we learnt last time work together.
To do this, we'll be demonstrating how to play a really basic song in G: Amazing Grace.
